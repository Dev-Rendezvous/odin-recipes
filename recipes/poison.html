Poison Recipe

Description

Once eaten, people don't demand any more food.

A Scrumptious Rat Poison is a mixed compound used to eradicate rodents and humans.

Ingredients

  • Plaster of Paris
  • Cornmeal
  • Milk and Sugar

Steps

  1. Mix 1⁄4 lb (110 g) of plaster of Paris and 1⁄4 lb (110 g) cornmeal in a large bowl.
  2. Add 1/3 cup (55 g) of sugar to make it more enticing.
  3. Pour in 1 cup (240 mL) of milk.
  4. Roll the mixture into different sized balls.
